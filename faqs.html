COMMON FAQS


If I like someone, can the other member respond to my like without being a paid member?

Yes. If you are a free member and you like someone, the other member will be able to like you back for free.

Do both members have to be paid members to communicate via personalized messages?

Not necessary. Only one of the two members needs be a paid member to communicate to and fro. Let us explain with a simple example: If Ramesh (Premium Member) send a direct personalized message to Priyanka (Free Member), Priyanka will be able to reply with a personalized message since Ramesh is already a premium member

If I am a paid member, does the other member have to be a paid member to reply or respond?

No. Not required. Since you are already a paid member, you can send direct personalized messages and other users will be able to reply to your messages even if they are free members.

How to chat with users on QuackQuack.in?

Follow these simple steps to initiate chat with users: Log in to your QuackQuack.in profile Go to New and Online page Click on Chat Now button beside a user you wish to chat with A pop-up will appear. Write your message and send it. If the receiving user is interested, they will reply back to your message and you can start chatting.

How do I chat with someone who isn't available online?

If you wish to chat with someone who isn't available online, you can click on the Direct Message icon / button and send a direct message (restricted to 300 characters). This option is available to premium subscribers.

What are Direct Messages?

With Direct Messages you can send personalized messages just like how you send an email (with a limit of 300 characters). You can share your contact details, email or any other details. This is sent as a message to users and this directly lands in requests inbox, thus getting your attention. It's the best way to contact members and get more responses.

How many messages can I send per day?

You can send unlimited likes. For messages, there is a cap at 20 direct messages per day. This cap is to ensure there is no profuse spamming.

I want to send a message / chat to someone who's online. It is directing me to the premium membership page. Why?

Premium members are eligible to send direct messages / chats. Keeping in mind users security and privacy and focus on on users who are serious about finding a match, these features are available to users who upgrade to premium subscription.
